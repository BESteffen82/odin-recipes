Sourdough Bagels

Homemade Sourdough Bagels

This recipe involves making sourdough bagels using a homemade sourdough stater. It will make eight bagels. You can add your favorite toppings as well. This recipe involves proofing the dough overnight so plan accordingly.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 100g active sourdough start
  • 255g water
  • 40g malt syrup
  • 10g sea salt
  • 500g bread flour
  • Everything Bagel seasoning

Water Bath

  • 6gal water
  • 1tsp baking soda
  • 2tbsp sugar

Steps

  1. In the bowl of a stand mixer whisk together the starter, water, salt, and malt syrup
  2. Add the bread flour and use a spatula to bring together the dough
  3. Add the dough hook to the mixer and knead the dough on low speed for 6-7 minutes.
  4. Transfer the dough to a lightly oiled bowl. After 30 minutes perform a set of folds.
  5. Repeat the folds every 30 minutes for the first two hours.
  6. Allow the dough to ferment for 6-12 hours until it has approximately doubled in size
  7. Place dough in the refrigerator to proof overnight
  8. In the morning pour the dough onto a clean work surface and shape eight equally-sized bagels
  9. Bring the water bath solution to a boil and boil each bagel for 15 seconds per side
  10. Remove the bagels with a strainer and place onto a parchment paper-lined baking sheet
  11. Sprinkle the Everything Bagel Seasoning onto the bagels
  12. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees and bake the bagels for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown
  13. Remove from oven and place on cooling rack to cool
