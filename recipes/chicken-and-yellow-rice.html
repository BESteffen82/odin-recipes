Chicken and Yellow Rice

Chicken and Yellow Rice Skillet

This recipe for chicken and yellow rice makes for a very quick and delicious dinner. It only uses one skillet and makes for a great weeknight meal

Ingredients

  • 4 bone-in chicken thighs
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup white Basmati Rice
  • 2-1/2 cup chicken stock
  • 1 tbsp tumeric powder
  • 1/2 tbsp lemon juice
  • fresh parsley
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
    • 1/2 tsp chili powder

Steps

  1. Combine the paprika, cumin powder, salt, and chili powder and rub generously onto the chicken
  2. Heat the oil in a large skillet and sear the chicken for 4-5 minutes per side
  3. Remove the chicken from the skillet. Add the rice,tumeric, lemon juice, and chicken stock
  4. Return the chicken to the skillet, bring to a boil, reduce the heat to low, and cover with a tight lid. Cook until the liquid is fully absorbed and rice is cooked through, about 20 minutes
  5. Turn off the heat and let it rest with lid on for another 5 minutes. Serve
